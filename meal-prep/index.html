What This Is

Not a Recipe Book.

I work a demanding career with long hours. I train hard. I have a young son and a wife I want to be present for. There is no window to cook during the week.

If the food isn't ready when the week starts, one of two things happens: I miss my targets, or I make bad decisions under time pressure. Neither is acceptable when you're trying to perform at a high level across your career, your body, and your family.

Discipline is not white-knuckling every meal. Discipline is building the system that makes good nutrition the default.

This system was built during a bodybuilding prep. It works for anyone. Whether you're tracking every gram or just trying to eat real food consistently — the process is the same. The precision scales to your goals. The infrastructure stays identical.

The Framework

Pick Your Level.

The system works at every level of precision. Your goal drives how tightly you track. The prep process itself doesn't change.

Entry Point

Consistent Eating

Not tracking macros. Stop eating fast food, start fueling with real food. Portion by eye. No scale required.
